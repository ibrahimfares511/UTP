About

UTP is specialized and fully dedicated to promoting and Supporting Oracle, Open Source Technologies, and Linux/Unix-based IT Solutions. Throughout the years, UTP Team has been expanding our partnerships and growing our portfolio of products and services to include solutions based on Oracle, Red Hat, Commvault, Veritas, and other hardware and software Leading technology providers. We accomplish this by using state-of-the-art methodologies, products, and tools, fostering a stimulating environment for the development of our consultants, and contributing to the societies in which we operate. UTP has delivered significant projects in oracle, open source, and Linux/Unix deep understanding and appreciation of each customer-specific use case, dedicated to new technologies and concepts like Oracle Database, MySQL Database, Postgress Database, Linux/Unix OS, and others.
